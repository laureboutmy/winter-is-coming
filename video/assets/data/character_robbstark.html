Robb Stark

Robb Stark, also called Young Wolf, is the King in the North. He is the eldest son of Eddard Stark and Catelyn Tully and the heir to Winterfell and the North. Upon his father's imprisonment for treason, he raises his banners and marches south with a host to aid his allies in the Riverlands and to free his father. After his father's execution, he is named King in the North and Trident by his followers.
